Classic Mint Chocolate Brownies

Deliciously Rich and Decadent Mint Chocolate Brownies

Mint chocolate brownies are a delightful twist on the classic chocolate brownie recipe. They're typically made with a rich, fudgy brownie base that's studded with chunks of semisweet chocolate and mint-flavored baking chips. The mint chips add a refreshing and slightly sweet flavor to the brownies, while the chocolate chips add richness and depth.

One of the key components of these brownies is the mint frosting. The frosting is typically made with butter, powdered sugar, milk, and peppermint extract. The butter and powdered sugar are beaten together until light and fluffy, while the milk and peppermint extract are added to create a smooth and creamy frosting. The frosting is then spread over the cooled brownies and chilled in the refrigerator to set.

There are a few different ways to incorporate mint into the brownie batter. Some recipes call for using peppermint extract or peppermint oil, while others use chopped fresh mint leaves or even crumbled peppermint candies. No matter how you choose to add mint to your brownies, the result is sure to be delicious.

Mint chocolate brownies are a popular dessert for holidays like Christmas and St. Patrick's Day, but they're also a great choice for any occasion where you want to indulge in something sweet and decadent. They pair well with a cold glass of milk or a hot cup of coffee or tea, making them a versatile dessert that can be enjoyed any time of day.

To make classic mint chocolate brownies, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp espresso powder
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Andes mint baking chips

For the mint frosting, you will need:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp peppermint extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan and line with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, melt the butter in the microwave or over a double boiler. Stir in the sugar, cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, espresso powder, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, stirring well after each addition.
  4. Add the flour and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and mint baking chips.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before frosting.
  9. To make the mint frosting, beat the softened butter in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy.
  10. Add the powdered sugar, milk, and peppermint extract, and beat until smooth.
  11. Spread the frosting evenly over the cooled brownies.
  12. Chill the brownies in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to set the frosting.
  13. Cut into squares and serve.

These classic mint chocolate brownies are perfect for a sweet treat or dessert for any occasion. The combination of chocolate and mint flavors is a classic that is sure to please everyone's taste buds.
