The espresso not only adds a bold coffee flavor to the brownies, but also helps to deepen the chocolate flavor and give the brownies a slightly fudgier texture.

Here is a simple recipe for espresso brownies:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 shots of espresso or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line an 8-inch square baking pan with parchment paper or grease it with butter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the melted butter, granulated sugar, and cocoa powder. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add in the eggs, one at a time, and mix until fully incorporated.
  4. Stir in the espresso or coffee and vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth out the top with a spatula.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let the brownies cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Cut the brownies into squares and serve.

Enjoy your delicious and decadent espresso brownies!
