Nutella Brownies

Nutella brownies are a popular dessert made with the rich and chocolatey Nutella spread. They are a decadent and delicious treat that combines the indulgent flavors of chocolate and hazelnut in a fudgy and moist brownie.

The basic recipe for Nutella brownies typically includes Nutella, butter, sugar, eggs, flour, and baking powder. The Nutella is melted with the butter to form a rich and creamy base for the brownies. Sugar, eggs, flour, and baking powder are then added to create a thick and gooey batter that is baked until it is set.

There are many variations of Nutella brownies that can be made by adding ingredients like chopped nuts, chocolate chips, or sea salt to enhance the flavor and texture. Some recipes also call for a swirl of Nutella on top of the brownies, creating a beautiful and delicious marbled effect.

Nutella brownies are a crowd-pleasing dessert that can be served at any occasion, from casual gatherings to formal events. They are perfect for satisfying a sweet tooth, and can be enjoyed warm or cold with a scoop of ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.

Here is a recipe for Nutella brownies:

  • 1 cup Nutella
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Optional: chopped nuts or chocolate chips for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8 inch baking pan and set aside.
  2. In a medium-sized microwave-safe bowl, combine the Nutella and unsalted butter. Microwave the mixture for 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until the butter is melted. Stir the mixture until it is smooth.
  3. Add the granulated sugar to the Nutella mixture and mix until it is well combined.
  4. Add the eggs to the Nutella mixture and whisk until they are fully incorporated.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the Nutella mixture, mixing until everything is well combined.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it out evenly.
  8. If desired, sprinkle some chopped nuts or chocolate chips on top of the batter.
  9. Bake the brownies for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  10. Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your delicious Nutella brownies!
